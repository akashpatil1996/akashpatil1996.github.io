Car Price Prediction

Car price prediction model is a useful tool for anyone looking to buy or sell a used car. There are so many factors to consider when buying a car, such as brand, model, year, kilometers driven, etc, it can be difficult to know if you're getting a fair deal. This is where this ML model can help you, it can help you determine the right price for a car based on its features. With an R2 score of 82%, it is a reliable and accurate model that can make the car buying process a lot easier. The model is trained on the Quikr Cars dataset present on Kaggle and uses linear regression to give its prediction.
